## I. ELEKTR YUKLAMALARINI HISOBLASH

Elektr yuklamalarini talab koeffitsienti ($K_c$) usuli bo'yicha hisoblaymiz. Sanoat iste'molchilari uchun talab koeffitsienti $K_c$ ko'rsatkichlarida berilgan.

1. Berilgan sanoat korxonasi uchun faol quvvat quyidagi ifoda bilan aniqlanadi:

$$P_{max} = P_{o'rn} \cdot K_c \quad [MVt] \tag{1}$$

bu yerda: $P_{o'rn}$ — korxonaning taqdim etilgan aktiv quvvati [MVt]; $K_c$ — talab koeffitsienti.

2. Yillik va kunlik yuklamalar jadvalini tuzish uchun to'liq va reaktiv quvvat aniqlanadi:

$$S_{max} = \frac{P_{max}}{\cos\varphi} \quad [MVA] \tag{2}$$

$$Q_{max} = P_{max} \cdot tg\varphi \quad [MVar] \tag{3}$$

### Iste'molchi 1 — Neftni qazib olish (6,3 kV tomoni)

Iste'molchining talab koeffitsienti $K_c = 0{,}65$, quvvat koeffitsienti $\cos\varphi = 0{,}84$, $tg\varphi = 0{,}6459$ ('Kc' va 'Kosinus' jadvalidan).

Topshiriq bo'yicha $P_{o'rn1} = 48$ MVt, chiquvchi liniyalar soni $n = 6$ ta, kuchlanish $U_k = 6{,}3$ kV.

$$P_{max1} = P_{o'rn1} \cdot K_{c1} = 48 \cdot 0{,}65 = 31{,}20 \text{ MVt}$$

$$S_{max1} = \frac{P_{max1}}{\cos\varphi_1} = \frac{31{,}20}{0{,}84} = 37{,}14 \text{ MVA}$$

$$Q_{max1} = P_{max1} \cdot tg\varphi_1 = 31{,}20 \cdot 0{,}6459 = 20{,}15 \text{ MVar}$$

### Iste'molchi 2 — Kimyo sexi (35 kV tomoni)

Iste'molchining talab koeffitsienti $K_c = 0{,}65$, quvvat koeffitsienti $\cos\varphi = 0{,}85$, $tg\varphi = 0{,}6197$ ('Kc' va 'Kosinus' jadvalidan).

Topshiriq bo'yicha $P_{o'rn2} = 49$ MVt, chiquvchi liniyalar soni $n = 4$ ta, kuchlanish $U_k = 35$ kV.

$$P_{max2} = P_{o'rn2} \cdot K_{c2} = 49 \cdot 0{,}65 = 31{,}85 \text{ MVt}$$

$$S_{max2} = \frac{P_{max2}}{\cos\varphi_2} = \frac{31{,}85}{0{,}85} = 37{,}47 \text{ MVA}$$

$$Q_{max2} = P_{max2} \cdot tg\varphi_2 = 31{,}85 \cdot 0{,}6197 = 19{,}74 \text{ MVar}$$

### Podstansiyaning yig'ma yuklamalari

Yuqorida hisoblangan maksimal quvvatlardan foydalanib podstansiyaning umumiy yuklama quvvatlari aniqlanadi:

$$P_{\Sigma} = \sum P_{max,i} \quad [MVt] \tag{4}$$

$$Q_{\Sigma} = \sum Q_{max,i} \quad [MVar] \tag{5}$$

$$S_{\Sigma} = \sqrt{P_{\Sigma}^2 + Q_{\Sigma}^2} \quad [MVA] \tag{6}$$

$$P_{\Sigma} = 31{,}20 + 31{,}85 = 63{,}05 \text{ MVt}$$

$$Q_{\Sigma} = 20{,}15 + 19{,}74 = 39{,}89 \text{ MVar}$$

$$S_{\Sigma} = \sqrt{63{,}05^2 + 39{,}89^2} = 74{,}61 \text{ MVA}$$

| Kimyo sexi (35 kV) | 31,85 | 19,74 | 37,47 |

## III. KUCH TRANSFORMATORINI SONI VA QUVVATINI ANIQLASH

Podstansiyaning iste'molchilari I va II toifali hisoblanadi. PUE talablariga binoan bunday iste'molchilar uchun kamida 2 ta kuch transformatori o'rnatilishi shart.

Transformator nominal quvvatini tanlash shartlari:

$$\beta_{norm} = \frac{S_{\Sigma}}{n \cdot S_{nom.T}} \leq 0{,}70 \tag{10}$$

$$\beta_{av} = \frac{S_{\Sigma}}{(n-1) \cdot S_{nom.T} \cdot 1{,}4} \leq 1{,}00 \tag{11}$$

bu yerda: $n = 2$ ta; $S_{nom.T}$ — bir transformatorning nominal quvvati; $\beta_{norm}$ — normal rejimda $\leq 0{,}70$; $\beta_{av}$ — avariya rejimida (1 ta transformer) $\leq 1{,}00$.

$S_{\Sigma} = 74{,}61$ MVA. Uch variant tekshiriladi:

### Variant 1: 2 × ТДТН-40000/110

$$\beta_{norm} = \frac{74{,}61}{2 \times 40} = 0{,}933 > 0{,}70 \quad \times \text{ Mos emas}$$

### Variant 2: 2 × ТДТН-63000/110 ← TANLANDI

$$\beta_{norm} = \frac{74{,}61}{2 \times 63} = 0{,}592 \leq 0{,}70 \quad \checkmark$$

$$\beta_{av} = \frac{74{,}61}{63 \times 1{,}4} = 0{,}846 \leq 1{,}00 \quad \checkmark$$

### Variant 3: 2 × ТДТН-80000/110

$$\beta_{norm} = \frac{74{,}61}{2 \times 80} = 0{,}466 \leq 0{,}70 \quad \checkmark$$

## V. QISQA TUTASHUV TOKLARNI HISOBLASH

Qisqa tutashuv (QT) toklari asosan elektr asbob-uskunalar parametrlarini tanlash yoki tekshirish, shuningdek rele muhofazasi va avtomatika qurilmasini sozlash uchun hisoblanadi.

Hisoblash nisbiy birliklar (o.b.) usulida amalga oshiriladi. Uch fazali simmetrik QT eng katta toklarni keltirib chiqaradi va asosiy hisoblash holati hisoblanadi.

**QT nuqtalari:** K1 — 110 kV shinasida; K2 — 35 kV shinasida; K3 — 6,3 kV shinasida.

### Bazaviy qiymatlar

Baza quvvat: $S_b = 100$ MVA. Baza kuchlanishlar:

$U_{b1} = 115$ kV (110 kV tomon); $U_{b2} = 37$ kV (35 kV tomon); $U_{b3} = 6{,}6$ kV (6,3 kV tomon)

Baza toklari:

$$I_b = \frac{S_b}{\sqrt{3} \cdot U_b} \tag{12}$$

$$I_{b1} = \frac{100}{\sqrt{3} \cdot 115} = 0{,}502 \text{ kA}$$

$$I_{b2} = \frac{100}{\sqrt{3} \cdot 37} = 1{,}560 \text{ kA}$$

$$I_{b3} = \frac{100}{\sqrt{3} \cdot 6{,}6} = 8{,}748 \text{ kA}$$

### Tizim (energosistema) qarshiligi

$$X^*_{sis} = \frac{S_b}{S_{k.z}} = \frac{100}{358{,}3} = 0{,}2791 \text{ o.b.} \tag{13}$$

### Uzatish liniyasi qarshiligi

Liniya: $L = 93{,}3$ km, sim markasi AC-185 (110 kV li liniyalar uchun standart). $r_0 = 0{,}162$ Om/km; $x_0 = 0{,}413$ Om/km.

$$R_{lin} = r_0 \cdot L = 0{,}162 \cdot 93{,}3 = 15{,}11 \text{ Om}$$

$$X_{lin} = x_0 \cdot L = 0{,}413 \cdot 93{,}3 = 38{,}53 \text{ Om}$$

$$R^*_{lin} = \frac{R_{lin} \cdot S_b}{U^2_{b1}} = \frac{15{,}11 \cdot 100}{115^2} = 0{,}1143 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$X^*_{lin} = \frac{X_{lin} \cdot S_b}{U^2_{b1}} = \frac{38{,}53 \cdot 100}{115^2} = 0{,}2914 \text{ o.b.}$$

### Transformator sariqlarining qarshiliklari

$$U_{k,Y}\% = 0{,}5 \cdot (U_{k,YO}\% + U_{k,YQ}\% - U_{k,OQ}\%) = 0{,}5 \cdot (10{,}5 + 17{,}5 - 6{,}5) = 10{,}75\%$$

$$U_{k,O'}\% = 0{,}5 \cdot (U_{k,YO}\% - U_{k,YQ}\% + U_{k,OQ}\%) = 0{,}5 \cdot (10{,}5 - 17{,}5 + 6{,}5) = -0{,}25\% \rightarrow |0{,}25|\%$$

$$U_{k,Q}\% = 0{,}5 \cdot (-U_{k,YO}\% + U_{k,YQ}\% + U_{k,OQ}\%) = 0{,}5 \cdot (-10{,}5 + 17{,}5 + 6{,}5) = 6{,}75\%$$

$$X^*_Y = \frac{U_{k,Y}\%}{100} \cdot \frac{S_b}{S_{nom}} = \frac{10{,}75}{100} \cdot \frac{100}{63} = 0{,}1706 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$X^*_{O'} = \frac{|U_{k,O'}|\%}{100} \cdot \frac{S_b}{S_{nom}} = \frac{0{,}25}{100} \cdot \frac{100}{63} = 0{,}0040 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$X^*_Q = \frac{U_{k,Q}\%}{100} \cdot \frac{S_b}{S_{nom}} = \frac{6{,}75}{100} \cdot \frac{100}{63} = 0{,}1071 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$R^*_T = \frac{\Delta P_k}{S_{nom} \cdot 1000} \cdot \frac{S_b}{S_{nom}} = \frac{245}{63000} \cdot \frac{100}{63} = 0{,}0062 \text{ o.b.}$$

### K1, K2, K3 nuqtalar uchun hisoblash

QT toklarini hisoblash formulalari:

$$I_{k} = \frac{1}{Z^*_k} \cdot I_b \tag{14}$$

$$i_{ud} = \sqrt{2} \cdot k_u \cdot I_k \tag{15}$$

$$k_u = 1 + e^{-\pi \cdot R^*/X^*} \tag{16}$$

$$B_k = I^2_k \cdot (t_{\Sigma} + T_a) \tag{17}$$

### K1 nuqtasi — 110 kV shinasi (Energosistema + Liniya)

$$X^*_{k1} = X^*_{sis} + X^*_{lin} = 0{,}2791 + 0{,}2914 = 0{,}5705 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$R^*_{k1} = R^*_{lin} = 0{,}1143 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$Z^*_{k1} = \sqrt{R^{*2}_{k1} + X^{*2}_{k1}} = \sqrt{0{,}1143^2 + 0{,}5705^2} = 0{,}5818 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$I_{k1} = \frac{1}{0{,}5818} \cdot 0{,}502 = 0{,}863 \text{ kA}$$

$$k_{u1} = 1 + e^{-\pi \cdot 0{,}2003} = 1{,}533$$

$$i_{ud1} = \sqrt{2} \cdot 1{,}533 \cdot 0{,}863 = 1{,}871 \text{ kA}$$

$$B_{k}(K1) = 0{,}863^2 \cdot (0{,}165 + 0{,}016) = 0{,}135 \text{ kA}^2 \cdot s$$

### K2 nuqtasi — 35 kV shinasi (+ T sariq Y + T sariq O', 2 ta parallel)

$$R^*_{k2} = 0{,}1143 + \frac{0{,}0062}{2} = 0{,}1174 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$Z^*_{k2} = \sqrt{0{,}1174^2 + 0{,}6578^2} = 0{,}6682 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$I_{k2} = \frac{1}{0{,}6682} \cdot 1{,}560 = 2{,}335 \text{ kA}$$

$$k_{u2} = 1 + e^{-\pi \cdot 0{,}1785} = 1{,}570$$

$$i_{ud2} = \sqrt{2} \cdot 1{,}570 \cdot 2{,}335 = 5{,}188 \text{ kA}$$

$$B_{k}(K2) = 2{,}335^2 \cdot (0{,}165 + 0{,}018) = 0{,}997 \text{ kA}^2 \cdot s$$

### K3 nuqtasi — 6,3 kV shinasi (+ T sariq Y + T sariq Q, 2 ta parallel)

$$X^*_{k3} = 0{,}2791 + 0{,}2914 + \frac{0{,}1706}{2} + \frac{0{,}1071}{2} = 0{,}7094 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$R^*_{k3} = 0{,}1143 + \frac{0{,}0062}{2} = 0{,}1174 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$Z^*_{k3} = \sqrt{0{,}1174^2 + 0{,}7094^2} = 0{,}7191 \text{ o.b.}$$

$$i_{ud3} = \sqrt{2} \cdot 1{,}595 \cdot 12{,}167 = 27{,}437 \text{ kA}$$

$$B_{k}(K3) = 12{,}167^2 \cdot (0{,}165 + 0{,}019) = 27{,}272 \text{ kA}^2 \cdot s$$
